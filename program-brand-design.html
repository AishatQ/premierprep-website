Brand Design Career Program

Learn to build brand identities and visual systems that look like an agency made them, because you'll learn to think and work like one.

What you'll cover

  • Brand Strategy and Identity: translating a business into a name, story, and visual position that holds up.
  • Logo and Mark Design: building marks that work at every size, from favicon to billboard.
  • Visual Systems: colour, type, and layout rules that keep a brand consistent across every touchpoint.
  • Design Tools: working professionally in Figma and Adobe, not just clicking around them.
  • Client Briefs and Presentation: reading a brief correctly and presenting design decisions with reasons, not vibes.
  • Brand Guidelines: packaging a system into a guide any team can follow without you in the room.
  • AI for Design: using AI tools for moodboards, iteration, and speed without losing craft.

Your 8 weeks

Week 0

Orientation and AI Essentials

Goal setting, community onboarding, and the AI foundations you'll use all cohort.

Weeks 1–6

Live classes and mini projects

Identity, logo, visual systems, tools, and client briefs, with weekly assignments, office hours, and peer feedback.

Week 7

Career Accelerator

CV, LinkedIn, portfolio, interview prep, freelancing, and client acquisition, for a design career specifically.

Week 8

Capstone and graduation

A complete brand identity, built and presented. Portfolio review, graduation, certificate.
